About

Hi! I’m Michelle,
a front end developer who enjoys crafting responsive, user friendly websites and web applications.

I’ve always been fascinated with computers and all that goes on behind the scenes to make software applications function effectively and efficiently. After reading my first programming book and applying those principles to run scripts and create websites, I was hooked. I learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery.

My love of computer programming propels me forward and my passion for learning drives me to dig deeper and reach higher every day.

When I’m not coding you’ll find me making pottery, taking an online course, or enjoying a relaxing cup of tea.
